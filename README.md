## Idea

This project is a frontend prototype of a Research Assistant that helps users read research documents, ask questions, organize notes, and gain insights using AI (mocked responses). The focus is on UI/UX design and product thinking, not backend AI implementation.

## Target Users

- Students
- Researchers
- Engineers and professionals working with technical documents

---

## Core Problems

- Difficulty understanding complex research material
- Scattered notes and lack of organization
- No single interface to read, ask questions, and save insights

--- 

## Pages & Flow

- Landing Page: Introduces the product and its purpose
- Dashboard: Shows recent activity and quick actions
- Chat / Ask AI: Ask questions related to documents
- Document Viewer: Read and highlight research papers
- Notes: Save summaries and important insights
- Settings: Manage user preferences
